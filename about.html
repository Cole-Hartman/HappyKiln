ABOUT US

What is Happy Kiln?

We are your trusted partner for kiln and pottery equipment repairs in Seattle and the greater area. As a dedicated kiln repair business based in the heart of Seattle, we specialize in servicing a wide range of kiln brands, including Crucible, Skutt, Paragon, Amaco, Olympic, Cress, L&L, Tucker's Cone Art, Even Heat, and more.

Our expertise is to ensure that your pottery studio operates seamlessly. With a commitment to excellence and a passion for the craft, Happy Kiln takes pride in offering reliable and efficient repairs for all makes and models. Trust Happy Kiln to keep your equipment in top-notch condition, so you can focus on your craft.
